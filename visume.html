1# Australia-Based Job Portal with AI-Powered Interview Features

This platform is a comprehensive job portal developed specifically for users in Australia. It allows employers to post job listings and candidates to browse and apply for available opportunities. Employers can access candidate profiles based on their subscription tier, enabling them to view detailed resumes, schedule interviews, send offers, or reject applications. The platform streamlines the hiring process by offering end-to-end candidate management tools. Candidates can search and apply for jobs, explore similar openings, and even participate in AI-powered mock interviews to prepare for real-life scenarios. These mock interviews provide real-time feedback, helping candidates improve their responses and boost confidence.

2# AI-Powered Mock Interview

Candidates can take an interview with AI, where the system will ask questions based on the job profile or interview type. After the session, a detailed result will be generated, offering insights into the candidate's performance and areas for improvement.

3# Google Calendar Integration

Google Calendar is integrated into the platform. When an employer schedules an interview or meeting, they can view the booked dates on their calendar. Similarly, the candidateâ€™s calendar will automatically update with the scheduled event, ensuring smooth coordination.

4# Zoom Meeting Integration

Zoom meetings are seamlessly integrated into the platform. When an employer schedules a meeting, the event will be added to both their Google Calendar and the candidate's calendar. The candidate can then join the meeting using the Zoom link provided in the event details.

5# Jora jobs thrpugh scraping

Additionally, the portal integrates job listings scraped from external sources such as the Jora website, ensuring a wide range of opportunities for job seekers. Employers can filter and select candidates based on desired qualifications, skills, and other criteria to make data-driven hiring decisions.

6# Subscription System

The application includes a fully integrated subscription system using Stripe for secure and seamless online payments. Users can choose between monthly and annual subscription plans based on their needs. Once subscribed, users gain access to premium features such as the ability to post a limited number of job listings, apply for available jobs, and view detailed profiles of potential employees or candidates. Additionally, payment can be completed via Stripe or by scanning a QR code using compatible digital wallets or bank apps, offering flexibility and convenience for users across various devices and regions.
